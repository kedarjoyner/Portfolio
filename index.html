Hello.

I'm Kedar — rhymes with cheddar — and I'm a creative stylist and front end dev intern at Dirigible Studio here in Madison, Wisco.

We live in a digital world where people spend less than 15 seconds on a web page if you don't capture their attention. I help discover and craft websites that engage and inspire everyday people through words, photography, and web design. Together we can help make the internet a beautiful place.

You can find me in the city, camera in hand, documenting the stories around me, or in little cafe's writing about my daily adventures and the people I meet.
